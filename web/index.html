How it works

Three steps to anything

From idea to done in seconds. No setup, no coding — just plain English.

01

You describe the task

Type any instruction in plain English — "Open Spotify and play my workout playlist" or "Find the cheapest flight to Nairobi next Friday".

02

Kazi sees your screen

The AI analyzes a live screenshot, identifies every UI element, and plans the exact sequence of actions needed to get it done.

03

It gets done

Kazi executes every click, keystroke, and scroll — checks the result — and continues until the task is fully complete.
