Capabilities

Everything a human assistant can do

KAZI combines screen vision with computer control to execute complex, multi-step tasks autonomously.

👁️

Screen Vision

Captures and analyzes your full screen in real-time to understand exactly what's on display before taking action.

🖱️

Mouse & Keyboard

Clicks, double-clicks, right-clicks, drags, types, scrolls — full computer control with pixel-precise accuracy.

💬

Natural Language

Just describe what you want done. No commands, no syntax — plain English instructions like you'd give a colleague.

🔄

Multi-Step Tasks

Executes up to 30 sequential steps per task, adapting to what appears on screen at each step.

🔔

System Tray

Runs quietly in the background. Press Ctrl+Shift+K anytime to summon KAZI instantly.

🛡️

Failsafe Mode

Move your mouse to any screen corner to instantly abort any running task. You're always in control.

How it works

Three steps to anything

1

You describe the task

Type any instruction in plain English — "Open Spotify and play my workout playlist" or "Find the best price for this laptop on Amazon".

→
2

KAZI sees your screen

Gemini 2.0 Flash analyzes a screenshot, identifies every UI element, and plans the exact sequence of actions needed.

→
3

It gets done

KAZI executes each action, checks the result, and continues until the task is complete — just like a human would.

Quick Install

  1. Download and extract the ZIP
  2. Double-click INSTALL_AND_RUN.bat
  3. It auto-installs Python + all dependencies
  4. Add your Gemini API key to .env
  5. Press Ctrl+Shift+K to open KAZI
